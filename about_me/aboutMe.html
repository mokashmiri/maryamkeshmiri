About Me

Maryam Keshmiri is a passionate sculptor whose work challenges traditional notions of beauty and meaning.

She draws inspiration from nature, daily experiences, and personal challenges. Maryam completed her Bachelor of Sculpture from Tehran University of Art and is currently pursuing a Master of Fine Art at the University of Oregon.

Her work focuses on creating dialogues between the viewers and the artwork, inviting them to reflect on their own assumptions and beliefs.
